# Getting Started

This guide walks you through setting up the Digital Mannequin for your store.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Supabase account (supabase.com)
- A Vercel account (vercel.com)
- Product images in JPG or PNG format
- A modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

## Setup steps

1. **Create your Supabase project** — this is your database and storage layer
2. **Upload your product catalogue** — images and metadata for each item
3. **Configure the mannequin** — set your store name and branding
4. **Deploy to Vercel** — make the PWA live for your customers

## What to expect

Setup takes approximately 30 minutes for a first-time retailer. Once configured, adding new products to the catalogue takes under 5 minutes per item.

:::tip
Start with 5–10 products to validate the experience before uploading your full catalogue.
:::
